What is Campus Tinder?

Campus Tinder is a website that allows students to find other students with similar interests. It is a great way to meet new people and make new friends. It is also a great way to find people to study with.

How does it work?

Students can create a profile and add their interests. They can then search for other students with similar interests. They can also search for students by college.

How much does it cost?

It is completely free to use Campus Tinder. We do not charge students to use our website. We do accept donations to help keep the website running.
